HISTORY

Palanas By The Sea in Boljoon, Cebu opened around 2016 as a seaside extension of the earlier Palanas Mountain Farm Resort, which originally began as a private rest house before being opened to the public. The creation of the beach resort allowed the “Palanas” brand to offer both mountain and coastal experiences for guests visiting Boljoon. Located near the Lusapon Bridge and easily accessible from the highway, the resort quickly became popular for its pool-by-the-sea concept, affordable day-use and overnight rates, and relaxed atmosphere. Over the years, it has contributed to Boljoon’s growing tourism by giving travelers another option alongside the town’s well-known historical and cultural attractions.

FEATURE LIFE STORY

Growing up in Boljoon, Violeta Estella often passed by the quiet shoreline where Palanas By The Sea now stands. As a child, she remembered the area as a simple stretch of sand and rock where fishermen docked their small boats at sunrise. When the resort opened in 2016, Violeta Estella—already working as a young receptionist—was one of its first employees. She watched the once-silent coast transform into a place filled with families laughing by the pool, travelers taking photos of the sea, and locals finding new job opportunities.

SUCCESS OF THE BUSINESS

The business in Palanas became successful because of hard work, patience, and good service to the community. Starting as a small store with limited products, the owner focused on meeting the daily needs of local residents by offering affordable prices and friendly customer service. Through dedication and strong community support, the business grew steadily and became a reliable source of income.

THE CHALLENGES FACED BY THE OWNER

The owner faced many challenges such as limited capital, low customer demand during bad weather, and competition from nearby stores. Managing expenses and supplies required careful planning. Despite these difficulties, determination and long hours of work helped the business survive and grow stronger.

PALANAS BY THE SEA IN 10 YEARS FROM NOW

Ten years from now, I see Palanas by the Sea as a well-known and trusted seaside resort in Boljoon that has grown in quality while keeping its peaceful and welcoming atmosphere. From a simple beach destination, it will have developed improved facilities, eco-friendly accommodations, and better services that provide guests with comfort and memorable experiences. I also envision the business as a stable source of livelihood for local residents and a contributor to sustainable tourism in the community. Above all, the success of Palanas by the Sea after ten years will be measured not only by profit, but by the trust of guests, the growth of the community, and the lasting value it brings to Boljoon.

HOW THE GOVERNMENT HELP THE BUSINESS GROW

The Philippine government has helped the growth of Palanas by the Sea through its support for tourism businesses by promoting local destinations, improving infrastructure, and providing training and accreditation. Agencies such as the Department of Tourism (DOT) help increase visibility by marketing coastal areas and encouraging both local and foreign tourists to visit places like Boljoon, Cebu. The government also supports resorts through DOT accreditation, which builds credibility and trust among guests, while programs from local government units help improve roads, facilities, and tourism services. These efforts make it easier for visitors to access the area, enhance the overall guest experience, and allow businesses like Palanas by the Sea to grow sustainably within the local tourism industry.
